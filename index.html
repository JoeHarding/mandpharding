About Us

M & P Harding are a traditional family business established since 1984, we can trace our connection with the construction industry back at least five generations. If you are looking for a personal touch for your project you can rest assured you will get it from us.

We can offer you a wide range of general building & hard landscaping services in and around the Buckinghamshire area, please browse our site to learn more and see how we can help you.

Services

Please see below a list of services we provide

General Building

new build, extensions, alterations, conversions, roofing, refurbishment, groundworks, renovations, conservatories, kitchens, bathrooms & property maintenance.

Landscaping

drives, decks, patios, garden walls, ponds, fencing, gazebos/pagolas, turfing & traditional hedgelaying.

Associated Trades

In addition to the above we also offer a full range of associated trades e.g. gas safe plumbers, part P electricians etc. including architectural, surveying & structural engineering services.
